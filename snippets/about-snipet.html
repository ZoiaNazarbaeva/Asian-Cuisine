About us


"Asian Cuisine" is a unique Japanese restaurant in Coimbra.

Restaurant "Asian Cuisine" is an integral part of Coimbra.

We have been in the gastro-business for 14 years and are not going to stop there.

Guests always make us happy, and as a token of gratitude and desire to see you again, we accrue 10% of the order amount as points to the bonus account for all guests - members of the Bonus Club.

You can spend them even on the same day! Up to 30% for delivery and up to 50% for pickup and check payment in the hall. Speaking of pickup! We have a 10% discount for him!

And if you have a birthday, then we will give you a 10% discount on delivery, pickup and restaurant visits. Don't be discouraged that it only happens once a year! After all, with us you can celebrate the whole week, ordering your favorite dishes at least all 7 days in a row!
